# QIP Registration Checklist

Use this educational scaffold to prepare for local QI or audit registration. Requirements vary by organisation.

## Project Definition

- [ ] Aim statement is specific and measurable.
- [ ] Project type is agreed: QIP, clinical audit, service evaluation, research, or another route.
- [ ] Clinical or operational sponsor is named.
- [ ] Patient safety risks are identified.
- [ ] Equality and access considerations are identified.

## Governance

- [ ] Local QI/audit registration route confirmed.
- [ ] Research ethics route considered if needed.
- [ ] IG/Caldicott route confirmed for any patient-level data.
- [ ] Data source owner identified.
- [ ] Output sharing route agreed.

## Measurement

- [ ] Outcome, process, and balancing measures are defined.
- [ ] Numerators and denominators are written down.
- [ ] Baseline period is defined.
- [ ] Review frequency is agreed.
- [ ] Re-audit or sustainment plan is recorded.

## Delivery

- [ ] First PDSA test is small and reversible.
- [ ] Staff affected by the change have been involved.
- [ ] Patient or carer input has been considered.
- [ ] Escalation route exists for safety concerns.
- [ ] Project closure and learning-sharing route are agreed.
