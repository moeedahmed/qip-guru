# De-Identification Checklist

This checklist is for synthetic or fully de-identified QIP and audit development work. It is not proof of anonymity and is not a local IG approval.

## Data Minimisation

- [ ] Use synthetic or fully de-identified data wherever possible.
- [ ] Remove NHS numbers, hospital numbers, names, addresses, phone numbers, email addresses, and exact dates unless locally approved.
- [ ] Avoid free-text fields unless a trained person reviews them.
- [ ] Replace exact dates with month, quarter, age band, or interval where possible.
- [ ] Remove rare roles, rare diagnoses, and unusual pathway details if they could identify someone.
- [ ] Check small cells before sharing tables or charts.

## Scanner Limits

- [ ] The scanner was used only as an assistive check.
- [ ] Names/free-text identifiers were checked separately or excluded.
- [ ] Findings were reviewed by a human.
- [ ] The source file remained in an approved location.
- [ ] The redacted output was checked before any sharing.
