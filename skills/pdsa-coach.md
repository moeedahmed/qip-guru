# PDSA Coach Skill

Use this static guide to structure a small Plan-Do-Study-Act cycle. Keep tests small, safe, reversible, and locally owned.

## Plan

- What exact change will be tested?
- Who is involved?
- Where and when will it happen?
- What data or feedback will be collected?
- What is the prediction?
- What safety or workload issue would stop the test?

## Do

- Record what actually happened.
- Note deviations from the plan.
- Capture staff and patient feedback if appropriate.
- Record any safety concern through the local route.

## Study

- Compare results with the prediction.
- Review outcome, process, and balancing measures.
- Check whether missing data or small numbers limit interpretation.

## Act

- Adopt: continue or spread the change.
- Adapt: modify and run another small cycle.
- Abandon: stop because the change did not help or created unacceptable risk.

Use synthetic or fully de-identified examples for practice. Confirm live-care changes with local clinical and operational owners.
