# Audit Standard Finder Skill

Use this static guide to organise audit-standard research. It does not retrieve or verify sources by itself.

## Source Hierarchy

1. Current local policy or SOP.
2. Current national guideline or standard, such as NICE, royal college, or specialty society guidance.
3. Trust, board, or integrated care policy.
4. Peer-reviewed evidence only when a formal standard is not available.

## Capture Fields

- Standard title.
- Owning body.
- Publication or review date.
- Exact recommendation or criterion.
- Expected compliance threshold.
- Inclusion and exclusion criteria.
- Evidence source for each data item.

## Review Checks

- Confirm the source is current.
- Confirm the standard applies to the local setting and patient group.
- Confirm the audit route with the local audit or governance team.
- Confirm data collection and storage with local IG if patient-level data is used.

Do not paste confidential local policy text into public tools. Use source titles, section references, and locally approved extracts only.
