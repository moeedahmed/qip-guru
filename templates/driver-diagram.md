## Aim

## Primary Drivers

1. Reliable identification of the target patient group.
2. Consistent delivery of the intended process.
3. Clear staff roles and escalation routes.
4. Data feedback that is timely enough to support learning.
5. Patient, carer, and equity considerations.

## Secondary Drivers

### Reliable Identification

- Agreed inclusion and exclusion criteria.
- Shared operational definition.
- Data source owner confirmed.

### Consistent Delivery

- Standard work or checklist.
- Training or briefing.
- Supplies, space, or digital workflow ready.

### Staff Roles

- Named clinical owner.
- Named operational owner.
- Escalation route for safety concerns.

### Data Feedback

- Measures defined.
- Collection frequency agreed.
- Small-number disclosure risk checked.

### Patient And Equity Considerations

- Patient experience input considered.
- Access barriers considered.
- Unintended consequences monitored.
