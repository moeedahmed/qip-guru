# Code of Conduct

QIP Guru contributors are expected to keep discussion professional, respectful, and focused on public-safe healthcare improvement tooling.

Do not post real patient, staff, organisation-sensitive, incident-identifiable, credential, or secret material in issues, examples, tests, discussions, or pull requests.

Unacceptable behaviour includes harassment, discriminatory language, threats, doxxing, and deliberate disruption of project work. Maintainers may edit, remove, or decline contributions that breach these expectations or the safety boundaries in `SAFETY.md`.

To report a concern, contact the project maintainer through the repository's listed maintainer channel. For clinical, information-governance, Caldicott, audit, ethics, or publication concerns, use the appropriate local route rather than this repository.
